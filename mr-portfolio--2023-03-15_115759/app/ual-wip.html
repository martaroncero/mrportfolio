UAL EXHIBITIONS


about project

The Work in Progress Exhibition poster design for London College of Communication has been created to promote the annual event showcasing the work of students from various courses. The design features a vibrant yellow colour scheme that evokes creativity and progress, with bold typography and imagery that grab the viewer's attention. The use of dynamic 3D shapes creates depth and visual interest and provides a glimpse of the talent and variety of courses represented. Overall, the poster design effectively communicates the excitement and diversity of the Work in Progress Exhibition, encouraging attendance and celebrating the creative output of the students at London College of Communication.

Client

University of the Arts London

Year

2019
