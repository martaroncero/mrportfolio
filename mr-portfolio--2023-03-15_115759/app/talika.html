TALIKA


about project

Talika brand is a well-known beauty and skincare company that has been in operation for over 70 years. Talika specializes in creating innovative and effective products that help individuals enhance their natural beauty. The goal of this project is to create a cohesive and engaging marketing and social media design campaign for Talika that will increase brand awareness, drive sales, and strengthen customer loyalty.

Email Marketing Design

Created engaging email marketing campaigns for Talika, resulting in increased customer engagement and sales.

Social Media Design

A series of eye-catching and informative social media posts that highlight Talika's products and benefits and digital ads that capture the attention of potential customers and encourage them to visit Talika's website.
