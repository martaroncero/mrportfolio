ZUTTA


about project

Zutta is a brand that seeks to redefine the boundaries between beauty, art, technology, and wellness. Their clinically proven devices and tools empower individuals to elevate their self-care routine and enhance their physical and mental wellbeing. As a designer, I was tasked with creating a visual identity for Zutta that embodies their bold and colourful approach to health and beauty. To achieve this, I drew inspiration from Zutta's commitment to innovation and creativity. The brand's identity features a vibrant colour palette that captures the energy and excitement of their products. The bold typography and graphic elements convey a sense of strength and confidence, while the playful patterns and textures add a touch of whimsy.

MARKETING

As part of this project, I also developed a range of marketing materials that showcase Zutta's products and their unique value proposition. From social media graphics and newsletter to landing pages, each element of the brand's visual identity is carefully crafted to appeal to Zutta's target audience and drive engagement.
