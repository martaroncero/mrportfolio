MARTA RONCERO

digital designer

I’m a multidisciplinary digital designer based between Madrid and London, working cross-disciplinary within the fields of visual communication and web design. I specialize in creating identities, campaigns, experiences, and more for beauty, culture, and lifestyle brands and organizations. I have an explorative approach whose pursuit is to create designs that translate and multiplicate into the digital space in a beautiful and accessible manner. I am especially interested in projects that are socially and environmentally sustainable.

branding

I specialize in branding design and work with clients to create visual identities that capture the essence of their brand and communicate it effectively to their target audience in a meaningful way.

UI / UX

Crafting intuitive and visually appealing interfaces that deliver a seamless and engaging user experience.

Marketing design

I also specialize in marketing design, creating targeted campaigns that leverage the power of design to drive engagement, conversions, and growth.
