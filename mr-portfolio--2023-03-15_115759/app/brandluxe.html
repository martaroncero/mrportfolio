BRANDLUXE


about project

BRANDLUXE is a brand that celebrates the intersection of beauty, technology, and design. Their mission is to curate inspiring and intelligent brands that offer both stunning aesthetics and practical functionality. As a designer, I was tasked with creating a visual identity that embodies the brand's earthy and calm approach to beauty and technology.

Company

Designed at Passiflora

Year

2022

Role

Brand Designer / UX Designer / Marketing Designer

BRANDING

I developed a color palette inspired by natural elements such as earthy browns, soothing blues, and calming greens. The typography is clean and modern, with subtle accents that add a touch of sophistication. The visual identity is designed to convey a sense of tranquility and balance, while still emphasizing the brand's commitment to innovation and cutting-edge technology.

UX / UI

To further drive engagement and sales, I created an e-commerce platform that is both user-friendly and visually appealing. The platform features a clean and modern design, with intuitive navigation and easy-to-use product pages. The checkout process is streamlined and secure, ensuring a seamless shopping experience for customers.

MARKETING

I also developed a comprehensive marketing design templates that leverages the brand's unique value proposition. BRANDLUXE is able to reach and engage with their target audience in a meaningful way.
